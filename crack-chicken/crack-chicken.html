Crack Chicken

This Slow Cooker Crack Chicken (Cheddar Ranch Chicken) is an addicting concoction of chicken, cheese, bacon and perfectly seasoned with ranch dressing. Cooking this in the slow cooker takes all the work out of it and makes it a super simple hearty dinner on busy days. Things are called “crack” for a good reason. They’re just so good you get addicted. You just can’t get enough!

Ingredients

  • Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts
  • Homemade Ranch Seasoning
  • Cream Cheese
  • Bacon
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Green Onion

Directions

  1. Slow Cooker: In the bottom of a slow cooker place the chicken. Sprinkle with dry ranch dressing. Cut the cream cheese into cubes and place over the chicken and seasoning.
  2. Cook: Cook on low for 6-8 hours or high for 4.
  3. Shred: Shred the chicken and top with bacon and cheese. Cover till melted. Garnish with green onions.
